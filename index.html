What we do

Full-Service Catering — We design a menu around your headcount and venue, then coordinate delivery, setup, service, and cleanup. Our pitmaster station carves ribs hot from the smoker for a made-for-Instagram moment.

Drop-Off & Setup — Budget-friendly. We bring labeled pans, chafers with fuel, and a layout guide so your team can host like pros.

Game-Day & Tailgate Packs — Rib racks, trays of Mac & Cheese, sauces by the bottle, and disposable warming kits sized for lots of hungry fans.

Fundraisers & Student Orgs — Profit-share nights and sauce-bottling workshops. We provide sign templates and QR codes for easy promos.

Corporate & Weddings — Timelines, staffing plans, rental coordination (warmers, cambros, buffet tables) and a rain plan. One contact; zero stress.

Our service promises

  • On-time delivery windows with live text updates.
  • Allergy labels and ingredient cards on every dish.
  • Backup fuel & utensils in every kit.
  • Leftover packing so nothing goes to waste.

How it works

  1. Plan — Tell us what you want, like something more casual or a wedding.
  2. Pick — Choose a package where you can add vegetarian or allergy options.
  3. Prep — We smoke low & slow so you get the timeline and shipping details.
  4. Party — We are the ones delievering, setting up, serving and cleaning.
