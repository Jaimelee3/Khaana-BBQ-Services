What we do

Full-Service Catering — We design a menu around your needs, then communicate all the way from the delievery to cleanup. Our chef station cooks up the best tasting ribs from the smoker for something you can post on social media.

Drop-Off & Setup — Cheap. We bring labeled pans, chafers with fuel, and a whole guide so your team can host without any complications.

Game-Day & Tailgate Packs — Rib racks, trays of Mac & Cheese, sauces by the bottle, and warming kits for all those hungry families.

Fundraisers & Student Orginizations — Profit-share nights and sauce-bottling workshops. We provide sign up sheets and QR codes to make it as convenient as possible.

Corporate & Weddings — Timelines, staffing plans, and rental coordination with warmers and buffet tables, making this a stress free wedding.

Our service promises

  • On-time delivery windows with live text updates.

How it works

  1. Plan — Tell us what you want, like something more casual or a wedding.
  2. Pick — Choose a package where you can add vegetarian or allergy options.
  3. Prep — We smoke low & slow so you get the timeline and shipping details.
  4. Party — We are the ones delievering, setting up, serving and cleaning.
